Every "Shop on Amazon" button takes you straight to that product's real Amazon listing in a new tab. You check out on Amazon, the way you always do — Luméra never touches your payment details.

No — prices are exactly what you'd pay going directly to Amazon. As an Amazon Associate, Luméra earns a small commission on qualifying purchases, at no extra cost to you.

How do you choose what to feature?

Everything here is something I've personally used or researched in depth — nothing is paid placement. If I wouldn't recommend it to a friend, it doesn't make the edit.

I need to return or track something I bought — can you help?

Since checkout happens on Amazon, returns, order tracking, and shipping questions are all handled through your Amazon account's order history, not through Luméra. Amazon's own customer service can help directly with any of that.

It's an AI assistant trained on this shop's products and policies — not Maryam typing live. It's great for quick questions about ingredients or finding the right product; for anything personal, feel free to reach out directly.
